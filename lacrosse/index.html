Lacrosse

Lacrosse was most popular in the late 1800s - early 1900s. Lacrosse gained popularity in educational institutions, leading to the establishment of vaeious Lacrosse leagues in schools and colleges. The sport continued to spread across Canada and the United States, as well as United Kingdom and Australia. Lacrosse was played in the olympic games in St. Louis and London. Lacrosse was featured as a demonstration sport three additional olympic games.
